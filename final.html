Colin's Final Project

Hi, this is my final project for my programming portfotlio. It is
a two player blackjack game where one person plays as the dealer and the other plays as the player.
The dealer acts as the dealer in a regular blackjack game, controlling who wins
and who loses by pressing the buttons on screen. The dealer lets the player go first and once the player stands,
the dealer may go and must stand on anything 17 to 21. If the dealer busts, player wins and vice versa.
If there is a tie, it is called a push and no one wins.
